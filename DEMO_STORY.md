**Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?**

A: Documents are versioned. When a new version is uploaded, the system maintains history and can compare versions automatically.

A: Yes - PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, CAD documentation, even scanned images with OCR.

A: All data stays within your network. No external AI services. Full access control by role and document classification.
